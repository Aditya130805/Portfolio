Future Goals
As mentioned above, the program can be improved further. In terms of accuracy and incorporating many more tweets definitely, but also in terms of the number of disorders it can detect. Currently, depression and anxiety each have been developed as binary classifiers - meaning the output is either depression or control OR anxiety or control. To take it one step further, with the seven different classifications of disorders present in the dataset, a multi-class classifier could be created. When made to run on the previous hundred tweets of a user, hence, the program will be able to more accurately determine which illness the user is suffering from too, picking up on the subtle differences between them.
